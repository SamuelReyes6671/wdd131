Peru

Peru (in Quechua and Aymara : Piruw ), officially the Republic of Peru , 1 is a sovereign country located in western South America . It borders to the north, with Ecuador and Colombia ; to the east, with Brazil and Bolivia ; to the south, with Chile ; and to the west, with the Pacific Ocean , which borders its coast. Its territory is made up of diverse landscapes: the valleys, the plateaus and the high peaks of the Andes unfold to the west towards the desert coast and the east towards the Amazon . It is one of the countries with the greatest biological diversity and greatest mineral resources in the world.
